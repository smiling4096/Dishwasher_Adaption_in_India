## Problem Statement

How can dishwashers be made a household necessity in India despite low adoption and strong reliance on manual dishwashing and domestic help?

## Context

- Dishwasher penetration in India is extremely low (<2%)
- High dependence on domestic help for daily chores
- Perception that dishwashers are expensive and unnecessary
- Limited awareness of long-term cost and hygiene benefits

## Objective

To identify key barriers to dishwasher adoption and propose practical, scalable strategies to increase their usage in Indian households.
